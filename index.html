Key Historical Events

Important milestones in Lebanese Forces history

1976 - Formation

Bashir Gemayel unifies Christian militias into the Lebanese Forces during the Lebanese Civil War.

Beirut, Lebanon

1982 - Bashir Elected President

Bashir Gemayel elected President of Lebanon, representing peak of LF political influence.

Beirut, Lebanon

1986 - Samir Geagea Leadership

Samir Geagea assumes leadership after internal power struggles, beginning long-term leadership.

Lebanon

2005 - Cedar Revolution

Samir Geagea released from prison after 11 years. LF re-legalized and re-emerges as major political force.

Beirut, Lebanon
