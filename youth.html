Who We Are

The Lebanese Forces Youth Movement represents the younger generation of Lebanon’s largest Christian political party. It serves as a platform for education, political engagement, and national awareness.

Through events, discussions, training sessions, and community initiatives, the youth movement prepares future leaders committed to sovereignty, democracy, and a strong Lebanese state.
