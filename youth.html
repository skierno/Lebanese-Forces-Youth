Who We Are

The Lebanese Forces Youth Movement represents the younger generation of Lebanon’s largest Christian political party in the USA. It serves as a platform for education, political engagement, and national awareness.

Through events, discussions, and community initiatives, the LF Youth USA prepares future leaders committed to sovereignty, democracy, and a strong Lebanese state through LF principles.
